RECYCLE

Recycling not only conserves raw materials, but it also protects the environment, saves energy, and reduces waste levels at landfill sites.

Pollution in ocean

Plastic pollution is a global problem that is growing exponentially due to both an increase in consumerism and an increase in the number of plastics used to manufacture the things we use on a daily basis.

80% of this plastic garbage comes from land, while the remaining 20% comes from marine sources, such as pieces of nets and other gear left in the water by fishing vessel fleets, according to the Environment agency of the ONU

Problematic

Latin America presents alarming and discouraging figures in the practice of recycling. No country exceeds 15% of recycled material for the garbage they produce per year.
